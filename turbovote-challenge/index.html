Help America reach 80% voter turnout by 2020.

Led by the nonpartisan, nonprofit Democracy Works, the TurboVote Challenge asks partners to use their scale and platforms to enable the goal of 80% voter participation by 2020. Participating companies show their commitment through the following: promoting voter registration and awareness among their employees, using communication channels to share The TurboVote Challenge with customers and stakeholders, sponsoring annual /semi-annual awareness campaigns to keep people engaged and voting in local and state elections, and encouraging civic related discussions and volunteer efforts.

Our Activities

Promote Voter Engagement

Regularly include voter engagement information in communications to primary stakeholder groups.

Technology Outreach

On web properties, promote technology that assists people with voter registration.

Press Coverage

Encourage press coverage of partners' roles in the coalition and broader civic engagement efforts.

Annual Campaigns

Design and execute an annual voter engagement campaign, ideally in September.

Stakeholder Engagement

Create opportunities (such as volunteering at polls or social media sharing) for stakeholders to take specific actions that build their civic-social capital.
